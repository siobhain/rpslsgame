How to Play RPSLS

You play against the device & you can start the game by clicking on one of the 5 buttons. There are 3 outcomes : win lose or draw according to the Rules below. You have 5 turns in the first round after which the results are displayed. You then have an option to play again & if you wish chose how many turns to have in the next round.
